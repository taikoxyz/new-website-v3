Based Contestable Rollups (BCRs) represent a new paradigm in Ethereum scaling. By combining the strengths of optimistic and ZK-rollups, BCRs deliver a modular, multi-proof design that maximally reduces costs and increases decentralization.

## The Multi-Proof Approach

Rather than relying on a single proof system, BCRs integrate a contestation mechanism that allows different proof systems to verify state transitions. This approach:

- Reduces trust assumptions
- Increases resilience against bugs in any single proof system
- Allows for progressive decentralization of the proving layer

## Based Sequencing

BCRs use Ethereum validators for transaction sequencing, making the rollup maximally decentralized from day one. There are no centralized sequencers or whitelisted operators.

## Looking Forward

Based Contestable Rollups are live on Taiko Mainnet today. We continue to iterate on the design, working toward a future where multiple proof systems compete to verify state transitions, driving costs down and security up.
