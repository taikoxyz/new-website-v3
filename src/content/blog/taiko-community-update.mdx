A look back at what the Taiko community has accomplished in Q1 2024 and what lies ahead.

## Highlights

- **Mainnet Preparation**: The community rallied around multiple testnets, helping us identify and fix issues ahead of mainnet launch
- **Ecosystem Growth**: Over 50 new projects joined the Taiko ecosystem
- **Community Governance**: Initial discussions around Taiko DAO governance structure

## Trailblazers Program

The Trailblazers program continues to grow, with community members earning rewards for their contributions to the ecosystem. From content creation to bug bounties, the program recognizes all forms of contribution.

## Looking Ahead

Q2 2024 will be focused on mainnet launch and ecosystem expansion. We invite everyone to participate in shaping the future of Taiko.
