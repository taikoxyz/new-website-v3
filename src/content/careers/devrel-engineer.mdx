## About the Role

As a Developer Relations Engineer at Taiko, you will be the bridge between our engineering team and the broader developer community. You will create educational content, build tools, and foster a thriving developer ecosystem.

## Responsibilities

- Create technical documentation, tutorials, and guides
- Build demo applications and developer tools
- Represent Taiko at conferences and hackathons
- Engage with the developer community on Discord, Twitter, and forums
- Gather developer feedback and relay it to the engineering team

## Requirements

- 3+ years in developer relations, technical writing, or software engineering
- Strong understanding of Ethereum and L2 scaling solutions
- Experience building with Solidity and web3 tooling
- Excellent communication and presentation skills
- Active presence in the crypto developer community

## How to Apply

Send your resume and examples of technical content you have created to careers@taiko.xyz.
