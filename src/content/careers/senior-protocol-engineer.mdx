## About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Protocol Engineer to join our core team. You will work on the Taiko protocol, designing and implementing key components of our Based Contestable Rollup architecture.

## Responsibilities

- Design and implement protocol-level features for the Taiko ZK-EVM
- Contribute to the development of our multi-proof system
- Collaborate with the research team on protocol improvements
- Review code and mentor junior engineers
- Participate in security audits and incident response

## Requirements

- 5+ years of software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Solidity and/or Go
- Deep understanding of Ethereum and rollup architectures
- Experience with ZK proof systems is a plus
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills

## How to Apply

Send your resume and a brief introduction to careers@taiko.xyz. Include links to relevant open-source contributions or projects.
