The Taiko ecosystem is growing rapidly, with projects spanning DeFi, NFTs, gaming, infrastructure, and more. Here's an overview of what's being built on Taiko.

## DeFi

Multiple DeFi protocols have deployed on Taiko, offering familiar services like swapping, lending, and yield farming — all with lower costs and the same security guarantees as Ethereum.

## Infrastructure

Key infrastructure projects including block explorers, RPC providers, oracles, and indexers are live on Taiko, ensuring developers have the tools they need.

## NFTs and Gaming

Creative projects are leveraging Taiko's low fees to bring NFT collections and on-chain games to life.
